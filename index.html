There was a Moment // Personal Thoughts

It all started with water.

a pre-ramble

There's a profound feeling to the world, I can only describe as connection. I find that this essence of interconnectivity between all things to be an incredibly powerful thing. There was a point where I realized just how important this connection was, a life changing experience of sorts created by an accumulation of circumstances which has led me to wanting to create this website. Although a website about connectivity sounded appealing, such a vauge topic could only lead me to find out how little I know about connectivity and how difficult it was to explain.

As I’ve worked and written a whole lot for this site I found the underlying importance of connectivity is its unifying nature. Understanding at its core, connection serves as the best explanation as to why we are here.

Ted Nelson describes his original vision of what the internet could have been, creating a tool that unifies us, helping us truly understand the sources of information we draw upon. The idea of the internet’s structure inherently incorporating this philosophy into the core of its functionality had such emotional appeal. Nelson describes his understanding of the world and his desire for others to share this understanding with others. At its core this philosophy he describes is the ability to connect.

How Did This All Happen // What History Left Behind

Humans, at our core, have not evolved that much.

“Once the human lineage had achieved a sufficiently large brain and had developed a sufficiently sophisticated culture (sometime around 40,000–50,000 years ago according to Gould, but more commonly placed at 10,000 years ago with the development of agriculture), cultural evolution supplanted biological evolution."

Our minds are essentially the same as people living in Mesopotamia and yet we find ourselves in such a technologically advanced complicated society. We can pity ourselves for not having the “simple lives” of farmers or hunter gathers, assuming life would have been much more freeing and easier back then but you have to imagine that what we have today was an inevitability of human curiosity. Hunting mammoths got old, intellectual challenges became far more satiating. Throughout all of time, there have always been dreamers and groups of people inspired to push the agenda. The remnants of history have shown us just how brilliant people were.

“You have to know the past to understand the present.”

In the modern age we can see why this retelling has become so important. A new age of connection, the internet progressing globalization at a rapid pase.

Global Connectivity or Connectography

Connectivity exists at every level of human evolution, in our day and age we begin to see this global connectivity heightened and exacerbated to levels we never thought were possible until the invention of the internet.

“Connectography represents a quantum leap in the mobility of people resources ideas but it is an evolution, an evolution of the world from political geography, which is how we legally divide the world to functional geography which is how we actually use the world.”

As we see this level of globalization form, a digital world largely without borders, where people move for work or school a great change for humanity on the horizon and we’re left with one essential quality that we all begin to see.

All of us are the same. We are all fundamentally human. We all have the same limitations, we exist as a species whose success depends on one another, we are social animals which have constructed the future we live in now.

Success of the Human Species // Connectivity as a Tool

Connectivity, connection, these are essential traits and the most powerful tool we collectively possess. When you make a connection with another person, you can feel it, it’s a powerful emotional tool we possess. The emotional capacity we have as people is what truly separated us from every other species and led to our success.

This ability to collaborate with others to create something greater than any single individual's skills, something greater than yourselves is a very unique human trait. Anything is possible with the right minds, circumstance and people.

collaboration, communication, cooperation= being human
